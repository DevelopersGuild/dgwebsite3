FAQs

Q: I'm a total n00b and don't have l33t hax0r ski11z. Can I still join in?

Yes! We work very hard to accommodate those who are just starting out. Try joining a beginner team!

Q: I don't have a laptop I can bring to the club meetings. Well, it's practically a potato. Do you provide computers?

Yes! In fact, our meeting room is filled to the brim with Windows PCs!

Q: I can't attend the entire meeting because I will arrive late/leave early. Will that be a problem?

It depends on your team. While some teams are very flexible, others may have trouble if members are unable to regularly attend meetings. In general, we try hard to be supportive of everyone, despite time constraints.
